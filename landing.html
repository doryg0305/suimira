Lip

How Suimira is different

Pre-set looks designed by celebrity makeup artists — you follow them.
You bring your own target (a filtered selfie you love) — we reverse-engineer the steps.
Virtual try-on: shows the look on your face. Doesn't teach you how to do it.
AR placement markers that follow your face — actual painting practice tool.
Western face geometry defaults, Eurocentric beauty bias.
Asian face structure first — under-eye triangle, nose bridge, eyelid type aware.
